About Me

My name is Yehia Ibrahim Saleh, born in Alexandria, Egypt, on August 13, 2002. I am a 22-year-old Software Engineer with a strong academic foundation, having graduated from Emirates Private School in Abudhabi, UAE , with an American High School Diploma in 2020, achieving a remarkable GPA of 3.92. I pursued my passion for technology by earning a dual degree in Computer Science from the Arab Academy for Science and Technology and the University of Northampton in 2024.

During my studies, I participated in the Egyptian Collegiate Programming Contest (ECPC) in 2023, which sparked my interest in competitive programming.This experience motivated me to solve over 200 problems on Codeforces and 100 on LeetCode. My goal is to achieve Grandmaster status on Codeforces and enhance my ranking on HackerRank.

I have a solid background in software development and project management, specializing in full-stack web applications, data analysis, and cloud-based solutions. With a keen interest in emerging technologies, I continuously seek opportunities to enhance my skills and deliver high-quality solutions.

Football has played a significant role in my life, teaching me valuable lessons in mental toughness, resilience, and effective communication. I played as a goalkeeper for Karama FC during my youth and most recently for Baynonah SC in the UAE U21 division in 2022. This experience not only helped me win numerous medals and trophies but also instilled in me the importance of teamwork, leadership, and handling pressure in competitive environments.

I am excited about the future and eager to leverage my programming skills to create impactful solutions that improve lives.

Hey Doc

Hey Doc is a medical chatbot designed to provide continuous medical assistance, minimize unnecessary doctor visits, and support individuals with social anxiety. The platform leverages machine learning, deep learning, and various APIs to offer secure mental health sessions, respond to medical inquiries, provide emotional support, and deliver diagnostic predictions. Built with Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it utilizes MongoDB for database management. Key frameworks and tools include Scikit-learn for machine learning algorithms, Pandas for data manipulation, Flask for backend management, Elevenlabs and Google APIs for text-to-speech and speech-to-text, PubMed API for medical article access, TensorFlow for deep learning, and Transformers for NLP tasks.
